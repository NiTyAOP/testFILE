Won 1st Prize in Maths (Science Exhibition 2018-19)

We feel privileged to announce that our school got selected to host 20th science fair in which 23 schools came up with innovative and creative projects. There were 69 exhibits with nearly 138 students involved in the event. Students had worked hard taking up the ownership of their research, set up displayed and presented their respective projects. Nirmal Shah from our school has secured first place in Maths Olympiad at district level. We wish him the best and proud of him. We thanks to respected judges, guests, principal and management for making this science fair successful. Our school students participated in Science Exhibition at Swaminarayan School Navsari on 28th August 2018. They were excited and very happy to present their projects. Students used scientific knowledge to present their experiment in an excellent way. The topics which were chosen by our students were extremely unique such as Power Of Pineal Glands, Trigonometric Identities by Super Hexagon, Zero Garbage etc
